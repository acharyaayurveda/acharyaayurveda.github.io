 When is the best time for panchakarma treatments ?

Karkidakam is a peak season for panchakarma treatments. But We recommend panchakarma any time starting from the rainy season in May till the end of January.

 What is special about Karkidakam ?

Karkidakam is called The Month of Rejuvenation. Ayurveda stresses the importance of ‘Ritu Charya’ or the diet and lifestyles to be followed in particular seasons to be free from the harmful effects of climatic changes. According to Ayurveda physicians, Kakidaka is the ideal period for rejuvenation treatments and therapies to purify the whole body and mind.
