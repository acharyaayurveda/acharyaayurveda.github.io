Ayurvedic Treatments

Acharya has expertise in all authentic Ayurvedic treatments, Panchakarma and traditional Kerala marma treatments. We advise treatments after a consultation and careful consideration of internal body composition and prakruthi. We manufacture Ayurvedic medicines and also are authorised distributers of Pathanjali medinices in Kerala.

Pizhichil : Medicated oil is poured onto the body in continuous streams while being gently massaged by therapists for 45 to 90 minutes.

Benefits: Increases vitality and immunity, good for clinical conditions like paraplegia, hemiplegia and other vata dominant ailments

Abhyangam: A full body deep tissue massage with specific herbal oils to nourish and revitalize the body tissues and to allow the toxins to be removed from the cells.

Benefits: Rejuvenates the whole body, beautifies the skin, delays ageing, reduces stress and induces sound sleep

Shirodhara/ Tailadhara: This treatment procedure involves a steady stream of medicated oil being poured on the forehead.  There are the specialized forms of Shirodhara called Thailadhara, Ksheeradhara, Takradhara and Jaladhara.

Benefits: Very effective for mental relaxation, sleep disturbance, headache, hair problems and insomnia. Used in the treatment of sinusitis, allergic rhinitis, greying of hair, neurological disorders, memory loss, insomnia, hearing impairment, tinnitus, vertigo, Ménière's disease and certain types of skin diseases like psoriasis.

Takra Dhara: Takra Dhara is the procedure wherein medicated butter milk is poured over the forehead in a steady stream

Benefits: Buttermilk has cooling properties and induces the same effect in the body and mind. It helps to relax the mind and reduce mental stress. Takradhara prevents greying of hair, reduces headaches, increases digestive power and alleviates anorexia.

Anti Cellulite / Powder Massage (Udvarthanam): This treatment procedure involves a fully body massage with a special herbal powder.

Benefits : The treatment of choice to reduce excess of body fat specially in areas where exercise takes longer to burn fat (such as hips, arms, chest etc). Beneficial in diseases like paralysis, imparts mobility to joints, strengthens muscles, reduces cellulite accumulation, improves circulation and enhances your skin's texture and appearance.

Navarakizhi: This treatment involves liberal use of medicated oils over the entire body of the patient followed by massage with small linen bags filled with cooked Navara rice.

Benefits: It is an immunity enhancing rejuvenation therapy that strengthens the nervous system, nourishes the tissues and improves the overall appearance of the skin. It is extremely effective against rheumatism, joint and muscle pains, back pains (e.g. sciatica, slipped discs), high blood pressure, high cholesterol levels, muscular wasting, arthritis, general weakness, paralysis, sore muscles, pains related to the joints etc.

Sirovasti: This is a specialized treatment that consists of holding the prescribed medicated oil at a bearable temperature in a leather cap fitted around the head of the patient. 

Benefits: Used for treating paralysis, insomnia, depression, in the treatment of disease of nostrils, mouth and throat, severe headaches, burning sensation of head, eye diseases, diseases of cranial nerves and hair fall. It is also helpful in improving memory, vision. Also effective in trigeminal neuralgia, hemicrania, optic atrophy, otalgia, deafness, facial paralysis and in all diseases affecting cranial nerves

Nasyam: Instillation of medicated drops in the nose

Benefits: Used in the treatment of headaches, acute sinusitis, frozen shoulder and hair-fall. It stimulates the brain cells and improves intelligence & memory power if practiced daily. Used to treat diseases like paralysis, Parkinson's disease, Bel's palsy etc. and other neurological diseases.

Elakkizhi: Herbal poultices are prepared with various herbs and medicated powder. Entire body is massaged with these poultices after they are warmed in medicated oils.

Benefits: Improves blood circulation and builds muscle strength. Relieves general body pain and stiffness. Beneficial in treating neurological disorders, arthritis, myalgia, chronic back pain, sciatica and spondylosis. Beneficial in osteoarthritis with swelling, spondylosis, sports injuries and all other soft tissue inflammations especially for joint pain.

Dhanyamla Dhara: A lukewarm medicinal recipe is poured over a steady stream all over the body or the effected part, with simultaneous gentle massage.

Benefits: It effectively works as an anti-inflammatory and analgesic. Its main purpose is the impact on subcutaneous fat, reducing excess fat and excess weight. Improves blood circulation, revitalizes the body, improves immunity and relieves body ache. A therapy for the treatment of conditions like swollen joints, hemiplegia, neurological disorders, arthritis, spondylitis, rheumatism etc
